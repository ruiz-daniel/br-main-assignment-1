My favorite book series

There is an anual event in my country called "International Book Expo" where books from almost everywhere around the world are sold at relatively affordable prices for the general public. Due to multiple reasons, is hard, borderline impossible, to officially buy most books from international authors here. However this one event offers some opportunities

When I was a kid my mother would take me there hoping I would get into reading

And it worked, during my childhood I spent a lot of time reading fantasy stories on paper

Out of nowhere, I was drawn in by these strange covers of teens turning into animals. And that's how I discovered my favorite series: Animorphs, by Katherine Applegate

A series about a group of teens fighting an alien invasion with the power of turning into animals and even aliens. I ended up reading around 20 to 30 of these for hours and some of them, multiple times

One thing I particularly loved about them was the writing in first person, making you feel in the mind of each of the characters

However the series goes way longer than that and I could never finish it. I should probably do it one day, but first I need a way to buy the rest of the books -_-

Of course this would take me to read other fantasy books and series

But as time went on, since adquiring books here isn't too easy and my other interests arose I became kinda separated from literature as I found mainly videogames and animation to be more expressive and somehow more accesible here

I still consider books an interesting and unique form of art and I hope in the future I'll have the time and proper conditions to engage with it again. Maybe when I can buy, and more importantly use, a Kindle :D

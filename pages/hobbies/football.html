My old passion with football and competition

Football is such a massive sport, that it was a matter of time it caught up to me.

However it was through playing a videogame , Pro Evolution Soccer 2022, that I became interested in it.

I liked the competition, the rivalries, and the game itself. So with my friends, from a very early age (7 or so), I started practicing it.

It became my main form of socialization, and of keeping myself healthy, since the other half of my free time would be dedicated indoors to videogames xD. I would often go play after school or during weekends, some times til quite late in the afternoon. I also remember gathering with friends to watch professional matches, bing a huge fan of AC Milan and italian football at the time.

Tho as the possibilities to watch football in my country were limited, and after getting into college I didn't have as much time nor the same ability to match schedules with my old friends, my interest dwindled and nowadays os more like a very nice memory

However, once born from videogames, it then mixed with videogames again, as my passion for sports went into the internet and to esports; competitive gaming

So nowadays I watch a lot of esports tournaments and play many competitive games myself
